FAQ

Questions members usually ask

This section helps with trust, objections, and overall clarity.

What is PathPay?

PathPay is a dashboard built to help members choose an AI service path, create a polished deliverable, understand how to price it, and know how to sell it to businesses.

Who is this for?

It is best for people who want a structured way to start selling AI-based services to local businesses without trying to figure everything out from scratch.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Most paths are built around copy, strategy, and delivery. The website path includes code, but it is still set up to be beginner-friendly and guided.

How does the Path Finder Quiz help?

The quiz helps members narrow down the best path based on what kind of work they want to do, what they want to sell, and what feels easiest for them to start with.

What gets generated inside each path?

Each path creates a more complete final product. That includes the deliverable itself, pricing suggestions, a sales script, steps to sell it, and bundle ideas.

Can I use this for different kinds of businesses?

Yes. The dashboard is designed to work with many local service businesses like barbers, salons, contractors, med spas, restaurants, cleaners, and more.

Why does PathPay feel more useful than just using ChatGPT alone?

Because it gives a workflow. Instead of just giving answers, it helps members choose the path, organize the business info, generate the service package, and move toward selling it.

What makes the dashboard trustworthy?

The structure, repeatable workflow, clear deliverables, built-in sales help, and member feedback sections all make the experience feel more reliable and easier to follow.
